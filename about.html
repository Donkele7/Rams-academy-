Our Story

Kelebogile Ramotebele, founder of Rams Academy, started the academy in 2025 after witnessing how poverty and crime robbed many talented children of their potential. Inspired by his own experiences, he assembled a team of passionate individuals to create a sanctuary of hope for young footballers.

With only donated footballs and a group of eager kids, Rams Academy quickly became more than a training ground. It grew into a movement that nurtures future leaders through mentorship, meals, safe transport, and professional-level coaching ‚Äî all for free.
